# Blueprint: Amigurumi Patterns eBook Landing Page

## Overview

A professional, single-page website to promote and sell an eBook containing over 4000 amigurumi patterns. The page is designed to be visually appealing, user-friendly, and optimized to convert visitors into customers. It also includes a dedicated section to attract and inform potential affiliate marketers.

## Design & Features

### General Aesthetics
*   **Color Palette:** A playful and crafty palette with soft pastels and vibrant accent colors to evoke creativity and charm.
*   **Typography:** A combination of a friendly, decorative font for headings and a clean, legible sans-serif for body text.
*   **Imagery:** High-quality, appealing images of finished amigurumi projects are used throughout the page to inspire potential buyers.
*   **Visual Effects:** Subtle drop shadows on cards and "glow" effects on interactive elements like buttons to create a sense of depth and premium quality.
*   **Layout:** A clean, well-spaced, single-column layout that guides the user logically down the page. The design is fully responsive.

### Implemented Sections
1.  **Header & Navigation:**
    *   A simple, sticky header containing the brand name "Amigurumi Dreams" and a primary Call-to-Action (CTA) button.

2.  **Hero Section:**
    *   **Headline:** "Descubre el Arte del Amigurumi con Más de 4000 Patrones Exclusivos."
    *   **Sub-headline:** "Tu viaje para crear adorables muñecos de crochet comienza aquí. Acceso instantáneo a un mundo de creatividad."
    *   **CTA Button:** A prominent "¡QUIERO MIS PATRONES!" button.

3.  **Benefits/Features Section:**
    *   An icon-driven section highlighting the core offerings for customers.

4.  **Image Gallery:**
    *   A visually engaging grid showcasing a variety of amigurumi that can be created from the patterns.

5.  **Final CTA / Pricing Section:**
    *   A clear, customer-focused section with pricing and a final purchase button.

6.  **Affiliate Program Section (New):**
    *   **Headline:** "Programa de Afiliados: ¡Gana Dinero con Tu Pasión!"
    *   **Target Audience:** Designed to attract and recruit affiliate marketers.
    *   **Key Benefits Highlighted:**
        *   High-converting, validated product.
        *   Generous commissions (70% - 80%).
        *   Dedicated affiliate support via Telegram.
        *   Complete sales funnel with order bumps to maximize earnings.
    *   **CTA Button:** "ÚNETE AL PROGRAMA DE AFILIADOS"

7.  **Footer:**
    *   Contains copyright information and social media icons.
